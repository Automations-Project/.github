# Automations-Project

Welcome to **Automations-Project** — a community dedicated to building, sharing, and improving automation workflows and tools.

## 🤖 What We Do

We create open-source automation solutions to help teams and individuals streamline repetitive tasks, boost productivity, and integrate services seamlessly.

## 📦 Our Projects

Explore our repositories to find:

- **Workflow automations** — GitHub Actions, CI/CD pipelines, and event-driven workflows
- **Bots & integrations** — Tools that connect and automate services
- **Reusable templates** — Starter kits and templates to jump-start your own automations
